Whispering Walls

A 6 months project, in a team with other 7 people. This graphic adventure was shown at Milan Games Week in October 2024. In this game, I had the opportunity to learn a lot about code refactoring and optimization, and I was able to fill many gaps in my coding knowledge. Furthermore, this was the first time I worked with external tools, such as Yarn Spinner for the dialogue system and Text Animator for text effects.
